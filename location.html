Location

Where to find us

Dordogne Language Studio is based in the historic town of Périgueux, at the heart of the Dordogne and within easy reach of the region's many treasures, including the prehistoric paintings of the Lascaux caves and the famous vineyards of Bordeaux, Bergerac and Pecharmant. Also close by are many charming villages such as Brantôme and the beautifully preserved medieval town of Sarlat-la-Canéda. 

Getting here

We can arrange for you to be collected from, and returned to one of the nearby airports of Limoges or Bergerac. Périgueux can also be reached by train from Bordeaux (one and a half hours, direct) or Paris (five hours, one change).
